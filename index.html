About

My start was in building compelling and innovative digital assets for small- and medium-sized companies. From product manager to designer to developer I covered most of the hands-on roles prior to moving into solution and enterprise architecture roles. Most of my experience in the last 10 years has been in architecting differentiating enterprise solutions for Fortune 500 companies with an emphasis on business process optimization and integration technologies. More recently I joined a start-up and now help companies become better at software development using Cloud Native Application Architecture technologies and techniques.

Portfolio

Large Utility Customer Management System

A large, regional energy provider initiated a project to integrate a new Customer Care and Billing solution with their ERP and other systems to increase internal productivity and ease-of-use for external customers. We provided general education around contemporary integration capabilities using the TOGAF SOA Reference Architecture and designed and installed the integration platform for a holistic enterprise integration approach. Follow-up education sessions provided the knowledge necessary for their internal team to become immediately productive.

Bank Check Image Software

My team was engaged by a leading bank check payment processing company to build a secure online check presentment solution for financial institution customers. We worked closely with the payment processor, financial institutions, and end users to create a highly functional online experience with multi-factor, layered security measures. The foundation of the solution included an API layer of HTTP microservices with a secure API gateway proxy. This application was developed over 10 years ago and continues in production today.

Retail E-Commerce Platform

A Top 25 retailer engaged us to completely overhaul their e-commerce architecture including the foundational middleware platform and packaged e-commerce software running atop it. This was a sensitive project with high executive oversight due to past instability issues during peak season. We phased in architecture and functionality changes over time with a series of transitions to the final state to provide consistency while increasing stability and scalability. This helped the retailer go through subsequent peak seasons with zero downtime.

I have led or been a significant contributor on hundreds of other successful projects, just ask for more information.
